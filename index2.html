Welcome to Marvel Wiki!
This page will help you dive deeper into the Marvel universe by giving you a detailed biography of all your favorite characters. In the Marvel Wiki, there is information on characters as popular as Iron-Man, and Captain American and those who are lesser known like 8-Ball.
For those who are new to the Marvel Universe, it is an American based company that started off by creating comics in October of 1939 with their publication of MarvelComics #1 featuring the hero the Human Torch, and anti-hero Namor the Sub-mariner. By 1941 Marvel was creating iconic themed characters such as Captain America. In 1961 we get into more of the modern comics during a time that Marvel Comics focused more closely on science-fiction works with titles like Journey into Mystery, Marvel dives into their own superhero team The Fantastic Four to compete with the DC Comics very successful Justice League. These series of events are what made Marvel into the highly successful company it is today, creating everything from TV shows, movies, comics, and much more. So, dive in and explore for anyone in the entirety of the Marvel Universe hero or villain in this dynamic database.
